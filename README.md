> *Pour your problems in. Walk out with answers.*

## Just want to use it?

👉 **[brew-audit.vercel.app](https://brew-audit.vercel.app)**

Type your name. Describe your business problem. Let Sage do the rest.
No sign-up. No setup. Nothing to install. Just open the link.

---

## What's in the cup?

Brew & Audit is a conversational AI business auditor. You describe a problem your business is facing. Sage — the consultant living inside — asks the right questions, finds the real bottleneck, and delivers a sharp, actionable audit. Then you keep talking until you know exactly what to do.

No forms. No uploads. No corporate jargon. Just a conversation.

---

## How do you order?

**You pour it out**  
Describe your business situation in plain language. The messier, the better. Sage is not here to judge — she is here to understand.

**Sage asks the right questions**  
She reads every word you said and asks 3–5 specific clarifying questions based on your exact situation — not a generic questionnaire. She wants real numbers, real processes, real people.

**You confirm the picture**  
Sage summarises what she heard. You confirm, add anything you forgot, or go straight to the audit.

**Your audit arrives**  
The real problem. What is slowing you down. What you can actually do about it. Where AI can replace the manual work. And one clear action for this week.

**Keep the conversation going**  
Ask follow-up questions. Go deeper on any recommendation. Download your full audit as a PDF whenever you are ready.

---

## What is Sage actually made of?

Sage is powered by **LLaMA 3.3 70B** via the Groq API — a large language model guided by prompts that shape her into a sharp, warm consultant rather than a generic chatbot. The prompts are the real craft here.

---

## The recipe

| Ingredient | Role |
|---|---|
| Groq API (LLaMA 3.3 70B) | Sage's brain |
| Vercel Serverless Functions | Keeps the API key hidden on the server |
| jsPDF | Generates the audit PDF in the browser |
| Vanilla HTML / CSS / JS | No frameworks — just clean, crafted code |

## Why was this brewed?

Business consultants are expensive, slow, and often speak in a language designed to sound impressive rather than actually help.

Brew & Audit is the alternative — a sharp, warm advisor available to anyone with a browser, any time, for free. The kind of conversation you wish you could have with a brilliant friend who genuinely knows business.

The café theme is intentional. The best business advice does not come from a boardroom deck. It comes from a real conversation over coffee with someone who actually listens.

## Want to run your own copy? (For developers)

Only relevant if you are a developer who wants to deploy their own version of this project.

1. Clone this repo
2. Create a free Groq account at [console.groq.com](https://console.groq.com)
3. Deploy to [Vercel](https://vercel.com)
4. Add your Groq key as an environment variable: `GROQ_API_KEY`
5. Done
